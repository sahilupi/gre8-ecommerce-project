About G8

G8 is a Delhi-based, multi-channel fashion brand, founded in 2020 by Lalhmangaihi Parte. Best known for its trend focused womenswear offering, G8 also has menswear.G8's collections are entirely designed keeping in mind the lates trends which started selling their products through WhatsApp and Instagram orders. G8 has received overwhelming feedbacks from the customers who are the Brand ambassadors as well now. After receiving positive and encouraging feedbacks, in 2023 G8 decided to reach out to wider audience by launching their website.

The range of goods sold online included middle-to-high-end clothing, beauty and accessories.
